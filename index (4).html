WFM Operations Command Center

WFM (Workforce Management) is the process of forecasting, scheduling, and optimizing workforce operations in real-time. This tool automates the full intraday WFM workflow for Real-Time Analyst (RTA) teams.

All logic runs in the browser — no server, no database, no installation. Open index.html and your command center is live.

Role Permissions

🔐
ADMIN
Full control: add/edit/delete tasks, manage breaks with overrides, log & escalate errors, archive & undo, PTO control, analyst management.
👤
ANALYST
Read access + one permission: mark assigned tasks as DONE. Admin tabs (PTO, Breaks, Archive) are hidden. All destructive actions are blocked.
🧠
AI ENGINE
Runs every 5s: auto-reassigns overloaded/inactive tasks, generates insights, calculates SLA risk and efficiency scores.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is WFM? ▾
WFM is the process of forecasting, scheduling, and optimizing workforce in real-time. In call centers: intraday scheduling, break management, task routing, adherence tracking, SLA compliance.
How does login work? ▾
Frontend credential check — no server. Sessions persist in localStorage so you stay logged in across refreshes. Admin: full control. Analyst: mark tasks done only.
Does this require a backend? ▾
No. Fully static. All logic runs in the browser via JavaScript. Data persists via localStorage. Works completely offline.
